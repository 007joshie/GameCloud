Gaming


Gaming refers to playing electronic games, whether the game is through either consoles, computers, smartphones. Gaming is a term that usually suggests regular gameplay, whether it be in free time or hardcore gaming and can't stop playing. Although it is regarded as a form of relaxation. Online multiplayer video games also known as MMO have made gaming a popular group activity as well. Because of the internet, players are about to play with other players from around the world at the same, a player could be in America and you could be playing in New Zealand and players can play together and face eachother.


Gaming is a major industry in Technology worth billions of dollars each year. Companies are investing resources into this industry and therefore attracting more people. The demand for more game programmers is growing because of the large industry. This is where we need more people to study computer science to that eventually people will get these jobs.
